    Religious Struggle
  • Muslims can struggle to keep a strong faith. In the West, most employers expect you to be at work for a full day everyday and they expect you to do your job. This can make it hard to pray 5 times a day and fast for Ramadan. In Muslim majority countries, daily prayer is encouraged and the work days during the month of Ramadan are shorter. EID (the last day of Ramadan) is also considered a holiday and paid day off.
