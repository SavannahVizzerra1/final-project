Challenges in the West

Muslims face a myraid of struggles when living in the West. From conflicted political feelings to religious practices, below I have noted a few common struggles.

  • Muslims sometimes have mixed feelings about Western governments. This is because countries like Britain and especially the United States, have a long, historical legacy of dipping their hands into the politics of Muslim majority countries. Not only have Western countries failed to understand the complexity of Islamic countries (I.e. the U.S. practically created ISIS and Al-Queda, both of which had no existence until the U.S. got involved), but they often times have done more harm than good and failed to respond to Muslim and government oppression abroad (I.e. Syria, Saudi Arabia.)
